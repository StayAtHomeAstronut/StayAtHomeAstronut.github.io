The Importance of Nature

In exchange for the information on this website, I ask that you treat these places with respect. On rare occasions, I may withhold information about a particular location to prevent it from being disrespected or desecrated by visitors. Irresponsible exploration is very detrimental to the delicate state of our unique ecosystem. By exploring responsibly and remembering to leave no trace, we can combat the damage done by inconsiderate visitors.
