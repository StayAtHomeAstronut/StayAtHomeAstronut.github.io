Plan an Adventure

Over the course of my adventures, I've come to learn that planning an adventure for yourself doesn't always have to be as daunting as it seems. With careful consideration, you can have a great trip with little cost, plenty of adventure, and minimal surprises. The reality is, you can plan an exciting adventure for yourself, see the things you want to see, and do it all for relatively cheap. The purpose of SeanDoesStuff is to connect you to the resources you need to plan an adventure, so that you can be free to see what you want to see and to make valuable memories.
