Places

I am constantly striving to find new adventures! If you're like me, you're probably looking for amazing places to see and experience. I categorize all of my photos and videos by where I took them, so that you can more easily find and experience those places for yourself. Some of the places I visit are easier to access than others. My adventures range from simple parking-lot views to strenuous back-country adventures. Whatever your cup of tea is, I designed this website for you. Here, you can find detailed descriptions of my trips, their cost, the supplies required, and how to obtain the correct permits, if necessary. I know well when it comes to planning a trip, advice from someone who has seen or experienced those locations can be valuable. My goal is to make these amazing places more accessible to those who want to experience them.
